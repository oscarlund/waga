COMING 2018

SHIRTS by WÅGA is a limited edition clothing concept coming 2018. Quality, expression and identity are always in focus. We want to create shirts that are unlike no others, and bring uniqueness back into fashion. Not everything needs to be mass produced to make you happy, right? Life is more than making profit. We want to create unique shirts that you want to wear. What makes you unique?

WHEN CAN I PRE-ORDER?

The pre-order will start in the fall of 2017. Only a limited collection will be sold during the first release. To make sure you will get one of the first SHIRTS by WÅGA, just become a member by signing up to our newsletter. By doing so, we will be able to notify you directly be mail, when the pre-order starts. You will also be able to take part in the development process, and selection of prints and colors for the first collection. We believe strongly in creation a balance between quality, sustainablitiy and personality in every product. By giving you access to the development of the new shirst, we believe you will be able to guide us towards creating the shirt of your dreams. Thank you!
